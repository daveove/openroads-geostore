The Geostore is a collection of tools to relay updated and usable information on the government roadwork projects.

The platform also serves as an online storage and management system of KML data project details. This endeavor is a result of the collaboration of multiple government agencies in an effort to visualize various roadwork projects and to better disclose government programs to the public.
